## Azgaar's Fantasy Map Generator

The small-town generation pipeline in
`client/src/generation/AzgaarTownGenerator.js` adapts the data-first generation
approach used by [Azgaar's Fantasy Map Generator](https://github.com/Azgaar/Fantasy-Map-Generator):
score habitable cells for settlement placement, connect important sites with
terrain-cost-aware routes, and keep generated world data separate from
rendering.
